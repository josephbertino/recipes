# Tamarind Glaze

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes  
**Cook Time:** 8 minutes  
**Total Time:** 13 minutes  
**Yield:** About 1/4 cup

## Ingredients

- 1.5 tbsp tamarind paste (or 2 tbsp tamarind concentrate)
- 1.5 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp fresh ginger, grated
- 1.5 tsp vegetable oil
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a small saucepan over medium heat.
2. Saut√© minced garlic and grated ginger for 30 seconds until fragrant.
3. Add tamarind paste, brown sugar, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and red pepper flakes.
4. Simmer for 5-8 minutes until thickened, stirring occasionally.
5. Strain through a fine mesh if you want a smoother glaze.
6. Use immediately or store in refrigerator for up to 1 week.

## Uses

This versatile glaze works well with pork ribs, chicken, duck, grilled fish, or roasted vegetables.
